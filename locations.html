Tourist visiting places in srilanka__


Sigiriya Rock
Location

  Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka.

Coconut Tree Hill
Location

  Coconut Tree Hill is presumably the most well-known place in Mirissa. You can watch the sea and Mirissa bay area from the top of this amazing place.

Yala National Park Safari
Location

  Yala National Park is the most visited and second largest national park in Sri Lanka, bordering the Indian Ocean.The park consists of five blocks, two of which are now open to the public.

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
Location

  Glorious Tooth Temple is a Buddhist temple in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It is located in the royal palace complex of the former Kingdom of Kandy.

Sri Pada / Adam's Peak
Location

  Adam's Peak is a 2,243 m (7,359 ft) tall conical mountain located in central Sri Lanka. It is well known for the Sri Pada "sacred footprint".

Galle Dutch Fort
Location

  Galle Fort in the Bay of Galle on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, was built first in 1588 by the Portuguese.

Sinharaja Forest Reserve
Location

  Sinharaja Forest Reserve is a forest reserve and a biodiversity hotspot in Sri Lanka.

Colombo National Museum
Location

  National Museum of Colombo, also known as the Sri Lanka National Museum is one of two museums in Colombo.

Adisham Bungalow
Location

  Adisham Hall, or Adisham Bungalow is a country house near Haputale, in the Badulla District, Sri Lanka.

Lake Gregory
Location

  Lake Gregory is a reservoir in heart of the tea country hill city, Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka.

World's End
Location

  World's End is located within the Horton Plains National Park in Nuwara Eliya District, Sri Lanka.

One Galle Face Mall
Location

  One Galle Face is a mixed-use complex of buildings currently being built near the Galle Face Green in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

National Zoo Gardens
Location

  National Zoological Gardens of Sri Lanka is a zoological garden in Dehiwala, Sri Lanka, founded in 1936.

Nallur Kandaswamy temple
Location

  Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil is a significant Hindu temple, located in Nallur, Northern Province, Sri Lanka.

Nainativu Island
Location

  Nainativu is a small but notable island off the coast of Jaffna Peninsula in the Northern Province, Sri Lanka.

Kanniya hot water spring
Location

  The Kanniya Hot Springs is a site with hot wells located in Trincomalee, Sri Lanka.

Adam's Bridge
Location

  Adam's Bridge is a chain of natural limestone shoals, between Rameswaram Island,India and Mannar Island, off the north-western coast of Sri Lanka.

Bambarakanda Falls
Location

  Bambarakanda Falls is the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka. With a height of 263 m (863 ft) it ranks as the 461st highest waterfall in the world.

Hummanaya
Location

  Hummanaya Blowhole is the only known blowhole in Sri Lanka and it is considered to be the second largest blowhole in the world.

Arugam Bay
Location

  Arugam Bay is situated on the Indian Ocean in the dry zone of Sri Lanka's southeast coast, and a historic settlement of the ancient Batticaloa Territory.
